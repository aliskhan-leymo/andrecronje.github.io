Code reviews are scarce nowadays with very few ICO’s actually releasing code, so instead I decided to publish some of my more standard review analysis.

In this review I will refer to my own point system, I will provide the formula in another post.

“The OPEN PLATFORM is a blockchain based developer platform centered around providing payment infrastructure for mainstream application developers to embrace decentralized technologies”

So they want to make integrating payment adoption with Crypto a lot easier. As someone that has integrated payment solutions for BTC, ETH, and a few alts, it’s not that hard. They like to compare themselves with Stripe API, integrating with banks is a lot harder than integrating with blockchain, so I’m not sure we are looking at the same niche.

Price

The basics, price of $0.08, 500m total sold, 1b total supply, $40m market cap. Low prices are good for market sentimentality, we can give them 1 point, and the low market cap is good for growth potential, we can give them 6 points.

Roadmap

They do currently have an MVP available and have a few demo’s on their website with their MVP 2.0 coming this month and go live in 07/2018, it’s a good roadmap and they should be able to get to developer adoption fairly quickly, we can give them 6 points.

4 points.

Advisors

Will Bunker, 500+, 99+ startups, 9, Operations Advisor
Lorne Lantz, 500+, 6 Bitcoin, 0, Payment Advisor (why?)
John Gardiner, 500+, 20 Financial Analysis, 0, Finance Advisor

Will is impressive, 2 point

Idea

As mentioned in the introduction, it’s a good idea, but adoption is currently really not that difficult for payments, so 4 points.

Community

Massive community (which I don’t understand), 1 point.

Conclusion

Developers are a good target demographic (although we don’t have money), making adoption easier for us is always a good idea, the development team seem very inexperienced but seem capable. The price metrics are good.
