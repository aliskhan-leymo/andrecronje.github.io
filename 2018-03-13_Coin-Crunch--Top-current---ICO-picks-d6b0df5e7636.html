Coin Crunch: Top current & ICO picks

A poll was taken in coin crunch telegram regarding everyone’s current top picks and their upcoming ICO pics. Since I was looking for new projects to review I decided to consolidate the list and share it here.

ICON won by a fairly clear margin, followed by WANCHAIN and NEO.

On the ICO side the list is a lot closer, NEX and Dfinity seemed to be tied (and this makes sense as they are the top 2 current contenders), what I didn’t expect was Sweetbridge and NuCypher fighting for the third spot, I expected NuCypher to easily take the lead.

Still, at least now I see what to look at next.
